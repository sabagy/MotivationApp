Privacy Policy for Motivation

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

Motivation is a daily inspirational quotes app. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

Motivation does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with third parties. All data stays on your device.

Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Quote preferences: Your category selections (e.g., Athletes, Managers) and like/dislike feedback to personalize future quotes.
  • Today's quote: The quote displayed for the current day, cached for the widget.

This data is stored in your device's local storage and is never sent to any server.

Widget

The home screen and lock screen widgets display the same quote as the main app. They read from the same local storage. No data leaves your device.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
